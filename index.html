About

What is Rice Savers?

We are a an AI company founded and created at HackNC 2022. Created with the goal of empowering farmers to spot disease, our company is taking on the task of creating AI solutions to the issue of disease. By simply submitting an image of your crop you suspect may have disease, our product can tell you if the crop is diseased and tell you the type of disease.
